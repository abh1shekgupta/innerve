  • BiOEZY is a new initiative to keep a check on the ever increasing waste disposal problem.
  • Herbivores pose a lot of threat to agricultural land. Removal of crop results in soil erosion, nutrient degradation, etc. This results in degradation of soil fertility.
  • To avoid such scenarios this initiative is introduced. Here we plan to recycle the waste introduced while farming to serve as food for herbivores.
  • The next step would involve collecting animal waste and processing it to make nutrient rich fertilizer.
  • This would create a loop to efficiently manage wastes at both end and provide an efficient way to avoid problems like soil erosion and land slides by avoiding crop uprooting.
  • This current initiative focuses on creating a loop to recycle waste
